Welcome to SolTown Acappella

SolTown is the official Germantown High School A Cappella Team, a competitive ensemble based in Germantown, Wisconsin.

A little nerding out about our name: "Germantown" (GTown) is a small village north of Milwaukee. In the fixed-do system of sightreading, "G" is called "Sol"—which ties back to our motto: "Always Dominant."

MidTown A Cappella

MidTown A Cappella is a dedicated group of Kennedy Middle School 6th–8th graders who perform popular songs with vocals and movement. Rehearsals are Thursdays in the KMS Choir Room from 4:45–6:00 PM.
